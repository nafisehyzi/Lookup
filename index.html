Meet Salman

Salman is the founder and lead physiotherapist at Look Up Physio. With over 10 years of experience in the field, he has helped thousands of patients recover from injuries and improve their quality of life.

His expertise spans across sports injuries, chronic pain management, post-surgical rehabilitation, and preventive care. Salman believes in a holistic approach to physiotherapy, combining manual therapy, exercise prescription, and patient education.
